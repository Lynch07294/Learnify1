Consciousness Studies Course

This course provides a comprehensive introduction to the study of consciousness. We will explore various scientific and philosophical perspective, including neuroscience, cognitive science, and phenomenology. Through lectures, readings and discussions you will gain a deeper understanding of the complex and fascinating nature of subjective experience.
